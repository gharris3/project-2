Covid-19 Guidlines

For the safety of our barbers and customers—we require you to follow these steps for service.
Masks are to be worn at all times for customers and barbers.
Temperature must be taken from each patron.
Hands of patrons and barbers must be washed before and after service.
Barbers will disinfect stations, tools, and capes between clients. So please be patient.
Beard trims will not be available at this time, due to state and city guidelines.
Please show up to your appointment alone as there are strict capacity rules.
If you’re feeling any cold or flu symptoms, please stay home.
